About The Webinar

On March 27, join your colleagues from across Ontario for a live webinar event! In this focus group, hosted by principals for principals, we will discuss FSL teacher retention. This 40-minute webinar is offered in two areas of focus, Core French and French Immersion/Extended French. The webinar will include both discussion and activity-based collaboration. Participants of the live event will receive free bonus materials. Follow #FSLOntarioLeaders on Twitter for updates.

Please register by March 23, 2018.
